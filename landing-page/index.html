About EcoTrack

Our Story

As students passionate about environmental sustainability, we created EcoTrack to help individuals understand and reduce their ecological footprint.

EcoTrack was born from this vision - a platform that not only tracks your environmental impact but also educates and motivates you to make better choices every day.

This project was developed as part of our ALX Portfolio Project (March 2025).
